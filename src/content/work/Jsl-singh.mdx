## About

JSL aka __Jaspal Singh__, is an Indian music director and singer from New Delhi, India. His track “Paranda” was Mega Hit, which made him famous. He started his Musical Journey with underground remix mixtapes in the early 2000s but due to lack of family support he left music and started assisting his dad in their family business, after a 2-year gap he decided to pursue his dreams to be a Music Director.
