## About

FoodBucket Waiter app allows waiter to take orders by simply selecting food categories and items with zero complexity.Customer can view the menu and place table top orders and the staff can deliver order to the table and collect payment once done. This mode is for bars and restaurants who like to adapt a work flow model with minimum staff and faster service.Get access to advance set of reports which cover sales, purchases, inventory, profit and loss statements. etc, which can be generated on daily. weekly. monthly. quarterly, semi-annually and annually basis.
